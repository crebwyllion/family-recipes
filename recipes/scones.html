Buttermilk Scones

Description

I don't have a picture, so I stole one that looks similar. Another family recipe, these scones are buttery with a nice crunchy outside and a soft inside. Makes 16.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup butter, chopped into small pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Topping

  • Milk
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Mix together all the dry ingredients with the butter.
  3. Add buttermilk and beat the mixture until it forms a dough.
  4. Pat it out on a floured board into a circle about 1 inch thick. Brush the top with milk and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
  5. Cut into wedges and bake for about 10 minutes.
