Buttermilk Scones

Description

I don't have a picture, so I stole one that looks similar. Another family recipe, these scones are buttery with a nice crunchy top. Makes 16.

Ingredients

  • Dough

      3 cups flour
      2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
      1/2 teaspoon baking soda
      1/3 cup sugar
      3/4 teaspoon salt
      1/3 cup butter, chopped into small pieces
      1 cup buttermilk

    Topping

      Milk
      Ground cinnamon
      Sugar

    • Steps

    • Preheat oven to 425 degrees.
      Mix together all the dry ingredients with the butter.
      Add buttermilk and beat the mixture until it forms a dough.
      Pat it out on a floured board into a circle about 1 inch thick. Brush the top with milk and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
      Cut into wedges and bake for about 10 minutes.
