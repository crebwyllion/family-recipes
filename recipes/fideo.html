Fideo

Description

I don't have a picture, so I stole one that looks similar. Another old family recipe (handed down through at least 4 generations!), this is a slightly more decadent take on fideo, using whole pasta and consomme. It's a nice way to dress up a classic comfort food. Probably serves 6.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 4 to 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 10.5 oz cans beef consomme
  • 1 package vermicelli or angelhair nests
  • 1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes
  • Sliced cheddar cheese for melting on top

Steps

  1. Using a large and deep pan, saute onions in oil until translucent.
  2. Add garlic and saute for 3 minutes, taking care not to burn the garlic.
  3. Move onions and garlic aside or remove from pan. Leave oil and brown coils. Browning coils on both sides adds to the flavor, so don't skip this part.
  4. Add consomme over the coils and return the onion mixture to the pan.
  5. Add can of tomatoes and let fideo cook until until the consomme is absorbed.
  6. Once the consomme has been absorbed, cover the top with cheese and let it melt.
  7. Eat it.
