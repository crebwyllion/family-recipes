Goulash alla Trieste

Description

I don't have a picture, so I stole one that looks similar. An old family recipe, this goulash is hearty and delicious with meat that falls apart in your mouth. Serves 6.

Ingredients

    • 2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
      2 large yellow onions, sliced
      1/4 cup olive oil
      1 cup beef broth
      1/2 cup red wine
      1/4 cup red wine vinegar
      1/2 teaspoon paprika
      1 teaspoon fresh sage
      1 bay leaf
      1 28oz can peeled plum tomatoes
      2 teaspoons salt
      2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper

    • Steps

    • In a large pot, put in enough olive oil to cover the bottom and brown onions over medium heat for 5 to 6 minutes.
      Turn heat up to high and brown the beef with the onions until there is no pink showing on the beef.
      Add the rest of the ingredients, reduce heat to low between a simmer and a low boil. Cook for 1 to 1 1/2 hours until the beef breaks up, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
